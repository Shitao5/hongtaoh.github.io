I graduated with a Master’s degree in Media Arts and Sciences from the Media School of Indiana University, advised by Dr. Nicole Martins. I earned my first Master’s degree in Journalism from Renmin University of China, Beijing. You can view my journalism pieces in St.Gallen Symposium Magazine. My research focus is on interpersonal interactions enabled by emerging communiation technolgoies. I am passionate about the future of human interactions, largely inspired by The Three-Body Problem, a famous Chinese sci-fi novel by Liu Cixin. I love R and Python.
